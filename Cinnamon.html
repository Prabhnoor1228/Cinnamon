My Recipes for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dought:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk wiith the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add the flour and mix until combined into a dought.
  4. kneal the dought for 10 min.
  5. Transfer the dought into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
